## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why is the video processing so much faster than other sites?
Most sites are limited by your **upload speed** (which is usually much slower than download speed). Because BladeTools skips the upload phase entirely, the "speed" depends on your computer's CPU and RAM, which is significantly faster.

### Q: Will my video quality decrease?
No. Our tools are designed to maintain the original bitrate and resolution of your source file unless you specifically choose to change the format during the conversion process.

### Q: Can I edit large 4K video files?
Yes, provided your computer has enough available memory (RAM). Since we process locally, the only limit is your own device's hardware, not our server capacity.

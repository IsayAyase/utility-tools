## 1. How to Resize Images and Convert Formats (WebP, JPG, PNG, ICO)
Different platforms require different image specifications. A high-res photo might be great for print, but it will slow down your website.

### Steps to Resize and Convert:
1.  Navigate to the [Image Resize & Format Converter](/tools/image/image_resize_convert_format).
2.  Drop your image (or multiple images for batch processing) into the tool.
3.  **Adjust Dimensions:** Enter your desired width or height. You can lock the aspect ratio to prevent your images from looking stretched.
4.  **Choose Your Format:** 
    *   Select **WebP** for the best web performance.
    *   Select **JPG** for universal compatibility.
    *   Select **ICO** if you are creating a favicon for a website.
5.  Click **Process** and download your optimized files immediately.

**Pro Tip:** Use this tool to compress large images before uploading them to social media or your blog to save data and improve loading speeds.
